  1. 14 July 2026, arXiv:2607.12528, Algebraic structures on non-Archimedean Urysohn universal metric spaces.
    I investigated valued-field structures on Urysohn universal ultrametric spaces.
  2. June/2026. My paper "An interpolation of metrics and spaces of metrics" is published in Topology and its Applications, and became Open Access ( link ).
  3. My paper "Spaces of metrics are Baire" became Open Access ( link ).
  4. The joint work with Katsuhisa Koshino "On isometric universality of spaces of metrics" is published in Topology and its Applications ( link ).
  5. My paper "Spaces of metrics are Baire" is published in Topology and its Applications ( link ).
  6. My paper "Characterization of Urysohn universal ultrametric spaces" is published in AGMS (link).
  7. 27th September/2024, arXiv:2409.17701 This is a joint work with Katsuhisa Koshino. We researched isometric universality of spaces of metrics. For examples, we prove that the space of metrics on the Cantor set admits an isometric embedding from arbitrary compact metric spaces.
  8. I am writing a manuscript titled "A survey on comeager subsets in spaces of metrics" for submitting to RIMS Kôkyûroku as an article based on my talk in "RIMS symposium (open), General topology and related fields, in June 2024".
  9. 4th July/2024, arXiv:2407.03030 , An isometric extensor of metrics.
    For a metrizable space, and its closed subset, an extensor of metrics is defined as a map from the space of metrics on the closed subset into the space of metrics on the space, which actually extends metrics. I constructed an extensor of metrics, which preserving the supremum metrics on spaces of metrics.
  10. Feb/2024, arXiv:2402.04565 , Spaces of metrics are Baire.
    I proved that, for an arbitrary metrizable space, the space of metrics on the space is Baire with respect to the uniform topology.
